Forests

Miyawaki Forests

Using the Japanese Miyawaki method, we grow dense native mini-forests that mature 10× faster, support wildlife and cool entire neighbourhoods.

25+ forests created
Plantation

Tree Plantation

Plantation drives and free saplings across schools, roadsides, temples and barren lands — each sapling nurtured until it stands strong.

85%+ survival rate
Seeds

Palm Seed Sowing

Sowing thousands of palm seeds along bunds and water bodies to recharge groundwater, prevent erosion and revive a vanishing tree.

10,000+ seeds sown
Water

Pond & Lake Revival

Dredging and rejuvenating water bodies such as Bedrapalli Lake — and planting memorial forests along their banks.

Memorial forests
Gardens

Vegetable Gardens

Setting up school and community vegetable gardens that teach children where healthy, chemical-free food really comes from.

School gardens

Awareness & Welfare

How We Work

Every project, three promises

1. Plan with the community

Local people choose the site, species and goals — so the project belongs to them.

2. Plant & nurture

We plant native species and care for them with water, mulch and protection.

3. Track & report

Geo-tagged photos and survival data, shared openly with every supporter.
